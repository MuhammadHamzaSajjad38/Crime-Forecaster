A technical overview of the data processing, modeling, and performance evaluation.

Our Approach

This project tackles the challenge of crime prediction by implementing a complete data science pipeline. Our methodology involved several key stages:

  1. Data Ingestion & Cleaning: We consolidated over 7 million crime records from the City of Chicago, handling missing values and dropping irrelevant columns.
  2. Feature Engineering: We extracted crucial features from the dataset, such as the year, month, day, and hour of the crime, and encoded categorical data like location descriptions into a machine-readable format.
  3. Model Training: We trained three diverse classification models: a Random Forest, a Multi-layer Perceptron (Neural Network), and K-Nearest Neighbors.
  4. Ensemble Modeling: To maximize accuracy, we combined the individual models into a powerful **Voting Ensemble**, which leverages the strengths of each classifier to make a more robust final prediction.
